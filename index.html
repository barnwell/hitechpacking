About Us

Our company is based on the belief that our customers' needs are of the utmost importance. Our entire team is committed to meeting those needs. As a result, a high percentage of our business is from repeat customers and referrals.

We specialize in all types of packaging, like fiberboard boxes for hazardous and non-hazardous shipments hazardous re-pouring of plastic drums, steel drums, issuing of dangerous goods declarations and labeling of hazardous material custom packing of painting, furniture, and other special material.

Hi-tech provides wood crating for our clients who prefer a stronger packing for more costly or valuable shipments. We also do dry ice packing for those time sensitive materials
